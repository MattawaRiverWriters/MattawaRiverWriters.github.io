Our Festival

The Mattawa River Writers Festival celebrates the influence of nature in literature with writing workshops, family eco day program, mindful hikes, meditative canoeing and a book talk.

Once a year, we celebrate Earth Day for as long as possible.

We provide an opportunity for emerging writers to work with storytellers, established authors and publishing insiders within a workshop format.

Additionally we deliver a family eco day program on the Saturday and an evening for adults with renowned Canadian writers.

Stay the weekend where the opportunity for burgeoning writers can come in contact with published authors in a nature setting. Hang out, play games at the campfire, wolf howl and walk through the gorgeous northern forests of Samuel de Champlain Park.

The Mattawa River is associated with indigenous history, the exploration of Canada and the fur trade. Let us introduce you to the history beneath your feet.
